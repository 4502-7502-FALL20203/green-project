The Top 7 Grossing Live-Action Films Directed by Women

While women have made significant strides directing box-office hit movies in recent years, men still dominate the industry. Not including animation, here are the top 7 grossing live-action films directed by women.

7. Black Widow (2021)

Director: Cate Shortland

Domestic Gross: $183.7 million

Natasha Romanoff, aka Black Widow, confronts the darker parts of her ledger when a dangerous conspiracy with ties to her past arises. Pursued by a force that will stop at nothing to bring her down, Natasha must deal with her history as a spy and the broken relationships left in her wake long before she became an Avenger.

6. Pitch Perfect 2 (2015)

Director: Elizabeth Banks

Domestic Gross: $184.3 million

After a scandal threatens to derail their last year, The Barden Bellas, the three-time defending champs worry that they've lost their harmony for good. With one chance left at redeeming their legacy, they must face off against the toughest competition on the planet: German supergroup Das Sound Machine and fight for their right to win at the World Championships of A Cappella.

5. Twilight (2008)

Director: Catherine Hardwicke

Domestic Gross: $194 million

A troubled teenage girl moves in with her dad in a small isolated town. She falls for a mysterious loner with a shocking secret; could he be anything to do with the local murders that have started taking place?

4. The Matrix Reloaded (2003)

Directors: The Wachowski Sisters

Domestic Gross: $281.6 million

Freedom fighters use extraordinary skills and weaponry to revolt against machines.

3. Wonder Woman (2017)

Director: Patty Jenkins

Domestic Gross: $412.8 million

When a pilot crashes and tells of conflict in the outside world, Diana, an Amazonian warrior, leaves her home, convinced she can stop the threat. Fighting alongside man in a war, Diana will discover her full powers and true destiny.

2. Captain Marvel (2019)

Directors: Anna Boden and Ryan Fleck

Domestic Gross: $426.8 million

Marvel Studios' Captain Marvel takes you on a spectacular adventure from the 1990s, tracing the path of Carol Danvers (Brie Larson) as she becomes one of the universe's most powerful heroes. When a galactic war reaches Earth, she meets young agent Nick Fury (Samuel L. Jackson) at the center of a maelstrom, leading to her ultimate destiny as an Avenger!
